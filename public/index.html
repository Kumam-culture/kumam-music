Terms & Conditions

Etokwa Music Artist Payment Agreement

1. Payment Registration
By registering for payments, you agree to provide accurate banking and mobile money details. Etokwa Music will use these details to disburse your earnings.

2. Earnings & Royalties
Artists earn UGX 0.001 per stream. Earnings are calculated monthly and disbursed upon request when the minimum threshold of UGX 10,000 is reached.

3. Donations
Artists receive 85% of all donations. Etokwa Music retains 15% as a platform service fee to cover payment processing and operations.

4. One-Time Registration Fee
The UGX 15,000 artist payment registration fee is non-refundable and covers account verification and payment infrastructure setup.

5. Content Ownership
Artists retain full copyright of their music. By uploading, you grant Etokwa Music a non-exclusive licence to stream and promote your content on the platform.

6. Prohibited Content
You must not upload content that infringes third-party rights, contains hate speech, or violates Ugandan law. Violation results in immediate account suspension without refund.

7. Account Suspension
Etokwa Music reserves the right to suspend accounts for policy violations. Pending earnings will be forfeited upon suspension for serious violations.

8. Updates
These terms may be updated. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of updated terms. Significant changes will be communicated via the notification system.
